Background Research & Problem Definition

The data, context, and real-world risks that define the problem NexLock One Touch is solving.

Background

Core Issue

Hundreds of thousands of children are injured each year in home accidents involving unsecured cabinets, accessible sharp objects, and tip-over furniture. These incidents are preventable — but many homes still lack reliable cabinet locks and anti-tip safety systems.

Root Causes

  • Cabinets storing sharp or hazardous items without consistent childproofing
  • Furniture and appliances left unanchored or improperly secured
  • Safety products that are bulky, confusing, or too easy to bypass
  • Low awareness of risk and injury frequency
⚡

Design Opportunity

NexLock One Touch targets a simple goal: make cabinet safety automatic, compact, and reliable — without complicated installs or parts that get lost. A better lock should work every time, in every home.
